# Definition
The ***centre of mass*** is the unique point where the mass appears to be concentrated.


For an object to balance the line of action of the centre of mass has to lie within the base

**Stable equilibrium** - after a small disturbance system will return to equilibrium
**Unstable equilibrium** - after a small disturbance system will ***NOT*** return to equilibrium.
