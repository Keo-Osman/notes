# Potential Difference vs Electromotive Force
*P.D* is the energy provided to each charge within the circuit. It is also the difference in potential between two points.
EMF accounts for the resistance within the battery it provides energy to the resistor in the battery.
$$
E=I(R+r)=V+Ir
$$
where $E$ is EMF, $R$ is the resistance of the circuit and $r$ is the internal resistance of the battery.

The *EMF* $\epsilon$ of a cell is equal to the electrical energy per unit charge gained by the charges as they pass through the cell.

$P.D<EMF$ as the charges lose energy as they pass through the cell.
